🧑‍🍳 Flavour Without Triggers
Garlic flavourInfuse oil with garlic cloves, remove cloves before eating
Onion flavourSpring onion greens, chives, asafoetida (tiny pinch)
Depth & umamiFish sauce, gluten-free soy, miso paste (1 tsp max)
HeatFresh chilli, bird's eye, chilli flakes, chilli crisp
RichnessButter, sesame oil, coconut milk (½c max)
Acid & brightnessLime juice, rice vinegar, lemon juice
🔄 Easy Swaps
Wheat noodlesRice noodles · glass noodles · vermicelli
Regular soy sauceGluten-free tamari or San-J soy
Regular milkLactose-free milk · almond milk
CreamLactose-free cream · coconut cream (½c max)
Bread / wrapsSourdough (2 slices) · corn tortillas
PastaRice pasta · 100% buckwheat soba
📦 Snack Prep Tips
Batch cookHard boil 4–6 eggs on Sunday — ready all week
Frozen barkYogurt bark keeps in freezer 2 weeks in a zip-lock
Energy ballsRoll on Sunday, fridge 1 week or freeze 1 month
Chia puddingPrep 4 jars at once — just top with fruit each morning
PinwheelsMake, wrap in cling film, slice day-of — 2 day shelf life
Smashed cucumberBest fresh, but holds dressed in fridge 1 day
