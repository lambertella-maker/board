The problem

I follow a low-FODMAP diet.
My recipe system was a mess.

How it works

A real conversation

You describe what you want. Claude builds it. You react in plain English. It updates. No code knowledge needed.

Me
I want a meal planning app for low-FODMAP eating. Tab bar layout, like a real phone app. I want to browse recipes by cuisine, match them to what's in my fridge, and use AI to generate new recipes from anything I find online.
Claude
On it. I'll build a mobile-first PWA with four tabs — Home, Recipes, Fridge Match, and AI Generate. Recipes tagged by cuisine and meal type. FODMAP rules hardcoded in: garlic-infused oil, spring onion greens only, sourdough bread. The fridge matcher cross-references your ingredient list against every recipe. I'll make it installable to your home screen so it looks and feels native…
Me (next session)
Can it automatically find a photo for each recipe? And I want the daily picks on the home tab to change every day but stay stable if I refresh.
Done. Added automatic photo lookup — checks Wikipedia first for canonical dish names, then falls back to recipe sites. Home picks are seeded from today's date so they're stable across refreshes, and change every morning at midnight.
What makes it cool

Features worth showing off

✨

AI Recipe Generator — paste anything, get a recipe card

Paste a dish name, URL, photo, or description. Claude reads it, adapts it for low-FODMAP, converts measurements to UK handfuls, and returns a formatted card. One tap to add it to your collection.

The most-used feature
🧊

Fridge Match — cook what you already have

Type the ingredients you have. The app cross-references every recipe in your collection and shows exactly what you can cook right now, ranked by ingredient overlap. Zero waste, zero thinking.

Works offline on phone
📸

Automatic recipe photos — every dish looks beautiful

The app automatically finds a high-quality photo for every recipe — Wikipedia for canonical dishes, then BBC Good Food, Serious Eats, and other sites as fallbacks. No manual uploads ever needed.

Fetched live, cached locally
📱

Installs to your home screen — no App Store

Open in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen. Installs like a native app, with a custom icon, no browser chrome, and works offline. The service worker caches everything so it loads instantly even without Wi-Fi.

iPhone, iPad, Mac
🌍

Lives on the internet — accessible from anywhere

Deployed to GitHub Pages (free). Has a real URL. No backend, no server bill, no login. A Cloudflare Worker handles AI calls so it works on any device — not just my Mac.

Free to host forever
