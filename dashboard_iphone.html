Anyone can buy in EU
No EU residency or ILR needed to purchase. US citizens, UK residents, anyone — hire a local notaire, get a local tax number, transfer funds. Your friend's mom bought in Salies exactly this way.
What ILR changes
Spending time there, not buying rights. Pre-ILR: trips >180 days/yr break your qualifying clock — own it but keep stays short. Post-ILR: spend months freely. Only limit: don't leave UK for >2 consecutive years or you lose ILR.
UK SDLT trap
Own anything abroad → lose first-time buyer SDLT relief + 3% surcharge on UK purchase. ~£12–15k extra on a £400k flat. Buy UK first, then EU.
