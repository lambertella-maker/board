ABBV Share Incentive Plan · How it works

Why this is good · £150/mo comes out of your salary before income tax and NI — so at your rate, ~£63 of every £150 is tax you never hand over. You effectively pay ~£87 in take-home for £150 of shares. AbbVie then matches 1:1, adding another free £150/mo. Total: £300/mo · £3,600/yr accumulating with zero action needed from you.
While you wait · Shares sit in a trustee account on a platform (check your AbbVie benefit portal to see it). You don't manage them monthly — just let them sit. Each batch has its own 3-year clock from purchase date: Nov 2024 → Nov 2027, Dec 2024 → Dec 2027, and so on. You can't sell before a batch's window opens.
When to sell · When a batch's window opens, log into the platform and sell — one transaction, not 60 monthly ones. Proceeds go straight to FSKAX via Wise. Sell most or all on each unlock: you're already heavily AbbVie through salary and pension, so you don't want extra exposure sitting here too.
